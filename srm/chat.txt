Information about College SRM Institute Of Science and Technology  SRM
SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) is one of the top ranking universities in India with over 20,000 students and 1,500 faculty, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health sciences, and Science and Humanities.
